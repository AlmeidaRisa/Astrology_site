The luxuries Of Venus

The soft beautiful planet popular for its ability to give luxury is a depiction of the Parashuram avatar of lord Vishnu. This was his sixth form. The story goes like this, once a Kshatriya king visits Parashuram's home, where he is not present at the moment and his father attends to the king and his army. The rishi (Parashuram's father) prepares the food for the king and his army very smoothly with the help of his cow, named Kamadhenu. Seeing how useful the cow was the king demanded that the rishi give it to him. Upon denying, the king killed the rishi by stabbing him 21 times and took the cow to his palace. When Parashuram returned home to this devastating event he killed the king. Then he went around the earth 21 times and killed every Kshatriya king who was troubling or torturing his subjects and appointed new kings in their place. That is why it is said that Venus makes you a king by giving you all the luxuries in your life. Also it is said that in order to make your Venus strong, it is a good practice to serve food and respect cows.
