Astrology

What is Astrology?
Astrology is believed to be the study of the movement of planets and also how they have an impact on or influence the lives of people including their career, health, money, family and marriage.

FUN FACT!

In Ramayan, the demon king Ravan controlled all the planets so that they wouldn't have any negative influence during his son Indrajit's birth.üò≤

Now, the movement of these 9 planets(note that each planet takes a different time to complete one rotation around the sun) determines in which next zodiac sign they will transit in and whether they will have a positive or negative effect in the person's life.
